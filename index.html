Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before starting your project with us.

Do you build with WordPress or Next.js?

Both. We recommend the best technology based on your project needs. WordPress is ideal for content-rich sites and teams that need easy editing. Next.js is best for performance-critical apps, headless architectures and dynamic web experiences.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely. Whether you have a WordPress site that needs modernising or a legacy codebase to migrate to Next.js, we handle complete redesigns and technology migrations.

How long does a project take?

A typical website takes 4 to 8 weeks from kick-off to launch. Complex applications or larger projects may take longer. We'll give you a realistic timeline during our initial consultation.

Will I be able to manage the website myself?

Yes. We build WordPress sites so clients can manage their own content easily. For Next.js projects, we integrate headless CMS platforms like Sanity or Contentful so your team stays in control.
